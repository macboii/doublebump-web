DoubleBump is committed to providing a safe environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) of any kind, and we take proactive steps to prevent, detect, and respond to it in accordance with Google Play's Child Safety Standards.

1. Our Standards

We strictly prohibit any content or behavior involving:

  • Child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE) in any form
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM), including AI-generated or synthetic depictions
  • Grooming — building trust with a minor for the purpose of sexual abuse, exploitation, or trafficking
  • Sexualization of minors, including profiles, images, or descriptions that depict or imply a minor in a sexual context
  • Solicitation, trafficking, or sextortion involving minors
  • Any attempt to use DoubleBump to contact, meet, or exploit a minor

2. Age Requirement

DoubleBump requires users to be at least 13 years of age. We do not knowingly permit anyone under 13 to create an account. Misrepresenting your age, or assisting an underage user in creating an account, is a violation of our Terms of Service and will result in account termination.

3. Reporting Abuse

DoubleBump provides an in-app mechanism for reporting abusive users, content, or behavior:

Report from the App Open a user's profile or an encounter, tap Report, and select a reason — including "Child safety concern"
Block from the App Immediately prevents further bumps, messages, or profile visibility from that user

You can also report a concern directly to us by email. Please include as much detail as possible (username, nickname, or bump time) so we can investigate quickly.

Report a Child Safety Concern

4. How We Respond

  • Reports involving CSAE or CSAM are reviewed and actioned as our top priority
  • Accounts and content confirmed to violate these standards are removed immediately and permanently
  • Confirmed CSAM is reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and/or relevant law enforcement authorities, in accordance with applicable law
  • We preserve evidence as required by law to support investigations
  • We cooperate with law enforcement and legal requests related to child safety
