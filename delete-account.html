Last updated: July 7, 2026

You can delete your DoubleBump account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how, both from inside the app and without it.

1. Delete In-App

  1. Open DoubleBump
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm — deletion begins immediately

2. Delete Without the App

If you no longer have the app installed, you can request deletion by email:

Send a deletion request Include the email or nickname associated with your account
Request Deletion by Email

3. What Gets Deleted

  • Profile — name, email, nickname, photo, social links
  • Bump history, relationship levels, and feed activity
  • Location data tied to bump events
  • Encounter memories and photos
  • Device identifiers and push notification tokens

4. Time to Complete

In-app deletion is processed immediately. Email requests are completed within 30 days. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
