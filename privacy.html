DoubleBump ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Data We Collect

Account & Profile

  • Name and email address — provided by Apple or Google at sign-in
  • Nickname — chosen by you during setup
  • Profile photo — optionally uploaded by you
  • Social media handles — optionally added by you (Instagram, X, LinkedIn, TikTok, Discord, GitHub, Spotify, Pinterest, Reddit, YouTube, Email)

Device & Technical

  • Device UUID — generated locally and stored in the device Keychain; used to support bump matching and device integrity
  • APNs push token — to deliver bump notifications
  • App version, OS version — for debugging and crash reporting

Motion Data (during bump only)

  • Accelerometer and gyroscope readings — captured for approximately 150ms during a double-bump gesture; used only to match your bump with a nearby device
  • Motion data is temporarily stored for bump matching and automatically deleted shortly after processing; it is never used for profiling or sold to third parties

Location (optional)

  • Location information associated with bump events — only when you grant permission and only after a successful bump
  • Location is used to display your social map; it is never used for real-time tracking
  • You can disable location recording at any time in Settings

Encounter Memories (optional)

  • Text comments up to 100 characters — written by you about a specific encounter
  • Photos — one photo per encounter, compressed and uploaded by you

Usage Data

  • Bump events, relationship levels, and feed activity — stored to operate your social graph
  • Ad interaction events — collected by Google AdMob when you view a rewarded ad

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate the bump matching and social graph features
  • To display your profile and social links to users you have bumped
  • To send push notifications about new bumps and level-ups
  • To show your bump map and encounter history
  • To serve voluntary rewarded video ads that unlock profile features
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

4. Data Retention

  • Account and profile data — retained until you delete your account
  • Bump event records — temporarily retained for matching, then deleted or marked as expired
  • Relationship and feed data — retained until you delete your account
  • Location data — retained until you delete your account or disable location sharing
  • Encounter memories and photos — retained until you delete your account

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Correction: View and edit your profile, nickname, social links, and memories in the App at any time
  • Location: Disable in Settings → Share my location, or revoke permission in iOS Settings → DoubleBump
  • Push Notifications: Disable in Settings → Push notifications, or in iOS Settings
  • Account Deletion: Go to Settings → Delete Account to permanently delete all your data. This action is irreversible.

6. Bluetooth

DoubleBump uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to detect nearby phones during a bump. BLE scanning is used only for proximity detection; we do not store Bluetooth identifiers or pair with other devices. BLE is not used when you are not actively bumping.

7. Children's Privacy

DoubleBump is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, contact us at bangcoderpro@gmail.com.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), Row-Level Security policies on our database, and access tokens with automatic rotation.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
